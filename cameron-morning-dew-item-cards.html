Tray · set A

Strange first. Purpose later.

Object
Cloth-Headed Weight
  • Dark-wood haft ending in a fist of layered, blood-tea–stained cloth
  • Smells of clove, sweat, and something sweetly medicinal underneath
  • Leather wrist-loop · head looks soft until you know better
  • Squeeze the cloth — yields, then meets a hard core that will not give
  • Swing through air — almost no whistle; the mass wants to arrive late and soft
GM: Mercy Mallet — sedate; drop one triage step.
Object
Singing Jar
  • Stoppered glass, mug-sized, heavier than empty glass should be
  • Inside: a dozen fat, segmented bodies with iridescent side-flaps
  • They leave wet silver trails on the glass · condensation beads wherever they climb
  • Warm hand near the glass — they surge toward the heat and begin a thin continuous eee, like wet fingers on crystal
GM: leech-kin — drain humors / fever-blood.
Object
Three-Fingered Crane
  • Palm-sized brass body grown around a spool of pale silk line
  • Line ends in three crystal prongs that look like a bird’s foot carved from ice
  • Prongs are cold and faintly sticky · the brass smells of oil and old storms
  • Turn the crank — the foot opens and closes: click, click, click
  • Open prongs near iron — one high, short ring, as if the crystal is hungry for metal
GM: extract foreign bodies / shrapnel.
Object
Warm Pot of Night
  • Lidded ceramic that stays warm when the room is not
  • Inside: black-green paste that stretches in living strings from a notched wooden spatula
  • Smell of wet leaves and barnyard-sweet · labels in three scripts, all deliberately smudged
  • Dab a little on skin — it grips like a mouth and will not wipe clean without oil
GM: sealant (fey dung resin).

Tray · set B

Strange first. Purpose later.

Object
Pale Cap with a Spoon
  • Soft white-gold mushroom, plum-wide, with a tiny wood spoon stained at the tip
  • Underside flutters if the air moves · stem weeps a clear bead if broken
  • Spores smell like crushed orchard fruit left in sun
  • Squeeze gently — a puff of fine spores; something in the ward wants to laugh when they land
GM: feed; laughter required for effect.
Object
Too-Long Knife
  • A blade the length of a short-sword pretending it belongs on a tray
  • Polish throws one hard white strip of lamp-light · full tang, leather grip with room to choke up
  • No blood groove — only faint hash marks near the ricasso, like a tally of something
  • Edge surgical-thin · spine thick enough to chop through what should not be chopped
  • Draw from the scabbard — one metallic rattle, then a silence that feels intentional
GM: amputate / cut away.
Object
Empty Bladder on a Vine
  • Transparent pouch, fist-sized when flat, hanging from a hollow living vine
  • Vine ends in a hollow bone needle · twist-cap at the top · nothing inside
  • Frayed neck-tag shows one crooked Common word: YES
  • Blow into the twist-cap — pouch swells, then sighs flat; the vine drinks the air toward the needle
  • Shake — a tiny dry rustle from the vine wall, as if something once lived there
GM: IV — player invents contents.
Object
Toothed Cuff
  • Hinged bracer of pale wood banded in brass, lined with felt pressed into a forearm’s ghost
  • Side-crank turns gears in a little window · too many teeth on the inner ring
  • Looks like jewelry that learned cruelty
  • Turn the crank — each click narrows the cuff a finger-width until whatever is inside cannot move
  • Open the hinge — sounds like a music-box run backward
GM: splint / set / brace.
