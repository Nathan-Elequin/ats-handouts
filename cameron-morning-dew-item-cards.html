Cloth Knob
  • Short dark-wood handle ending in a fist-sized cloth bundle
  • Many stained layers · leather loop at the butt
  • Smell the cloth — clove and old sweat
  • Squeeze the head — gives slightly; hard core underneath
  • Swing through the air — almost no whistle; soft, slow
  • Thump your own palm — solid thud, cloth mutes the sting
GM: Mercy Mallet — sedate; drop one triage step.
Object
Glass Vessel
  • Clear, stoppered · large-mug size · heavier than it looks
  • Condensation beads inside
  • ~12 fat segmented creatures, finger-long, iridescent side-flaps
  • Warm hand near the glass — they climb toward you
  • Hold still and listen — thin continuous eee, wet fingers on crystal
  • Tilt the vessel — they slide as one sticky mass, then re-stick
GM: leech-kin — drain humors / fever-blood.
Object
Brass Box
  • Palm-sized · crank on one side · pale silk line on a spool
  • Line ends in three crystal prongs
  • Turn the crank — prongs open and close: click, click, click
  • Touch a prong — cold, slightly sticky
  • Point the open prongs at iron nails — one high, short ring
  • Hold with both hands — crank + steady the body; line wants to twist
GM: extract foreign bodies / shrapnel.
Object
Ceramic Pot
  • Lidded · stays warm when the room is not
  • Black-green paste · wooden spatula notched by years of grip
  • Labels in three scripts, all smudged
  • Lift the lid — wet leaves + barnyard-sweet
  • Lift paste on the spatula — stretches in strings, then snaps
  • Dab a little on skin — grips; will not wipe clean without oil
GM: sealant (fey dung resin).

  • Catch lamp-light on the face — one hard white strip
  • Draw from the scabbard — one metallic rattle, then quiet
  • Choke up with a second hand — grip has room; balance shifts forward
  • Press the flat to your forearm — cold sinks in fast
