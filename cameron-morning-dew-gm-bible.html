Ambervale Field Hospital

Quack / The Morning Dew · Cameron Red Cap · ~3 hours · Items obfuscated · Deal → Throw → Claim

What changed (v2)

  • Items: Fantastical, generic, intentionally vague. Physical look cards only — purpose learned by testing on patients (humor + puzzle).
  • The Stick: Universal / innate — not part of the 8. Mythically oversold. Optional fake “charge” die that does nothing useful.
  • Teach beat: Cameron: “Find your stick. Figure the rest out. No time.”
  • Round loop: Deal hands → 30s read → countdown → Throw → Claim (anti-hoard) → treat.
  • Patients: Only ~4 soldiers; rest from scattered Feywild locales (future recognition). Lovers are not soldiers.
  • Triage names: Green / Gold / Scarlet / Ink (shoutable; MCI-homage without Pearldew).

Design note: why not “keep your two”?

If players may keep during the snap, optimal play is calm coordination: everyone holds two, chaos dies.

Rule (locked for v2): Throw → Claim.

  1. Deal patient cards (often uneven on purpose: e.g. 3 / 3 / 2 / 2).
  2. 30 seconds to read patients aloud/quietly. Item cards stay face-up at seats.
  3. Countdown from 10. On zero: everyone simultaneously puts all patients into the center (no keeping).
  4. Immediately: Claim — each healer may take up to 2. If two hands hit the same card, it goes to Overflow (untreated this round; Fear magnet).
  5. Cards left in the pile after claim = untreated.

Coordination becomes reading the table in 30s, then a messy grab — not a polite keep. Overstuffing still happens when two people dive for the “easy” patient.

Softer alt (if Claim feels too mean): Must-Pass-One — if you were dealt ≥2, you must throw at least one before claim. Prefer Throw→Claim first.

Round structure

  1. Fear / Dew / blood bookkeeping from last round.
  2. Deal from the Active stack (ramp: after 4 Saved, keep ~10 in the ecosystem).
  3. 30s read → 10-count → Throw → Claim.
  4. Each healer with 1–2 patients: creative use of Stick + seat items + own abilities; Duality rolls.
  5. Cameron may Bark Advice / Cap Transfusion / Never Rest as before.
  6. Modules (break room, lovers, Dart EMP, climax) unchanged in intent.

Triage (renamed)

Green — holding / minor Gold — waiting / delayed Scarlet — urgent / immediate Ink — beyond / expectant

Ink ≈ impossible PC DC (~40). Cap Transfusion +~20, costs 1 blood pip (bank of 4).

Improve one step on success; Saved removes from play.

The Stick (universal)

Every healer forages / is “chosen by” a plain wood stick within five feet of the ward. Cameron hyperexaggerates it: wisdom-on-contact, medicinal destiny, never for sale.

Truth: it’s a poking stick. Diagnosis via poke. Optional prop: d6 “charges” that count down on use — nothing happens at zero except maybe it snaps after the 6th poke (comedy).

Not one of the eight seat items.

Eight seat items (obfuscated)

Place two item cards at each of four seats before play. Players grab nearest. Do not explain purpose. Print look-cards only (see teaching/item kit).

Patients

14 total. ~4 court soldiers (plot: both courts leave wounded). Rest: civilians / travelers from map locales (Ambervale environs, Pale Ring fallout adjacent, Gray Reach road, Summer fringe villages, Gloaming mist-hamlets, river folk, mushroom pilgrims). Lovers = non-soldiers from different regions.

Reward later: returning to a locale and recognizing someone they saved.
