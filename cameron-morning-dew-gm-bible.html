Ambervale Field Hospital

Lann: The Morning Dew (Experience: Quack) · Guest: Cameron (Red Cap) · Spotlight: Edmund · ~3 hours · Canon override Jul 17 2026

1. Encounter brief (canon freeze)

Sensory: Care, confidence, smells of herbs and spices.

Who: A Red Cap field doctor who already held Quack before the party arrives. Care + confidence became absolute: I can save everyone. I will. He soaks his cap in his own life-blood to power healing — flipped folklore (gauze/transfusion, not murder-trophy).

Where: Ambervale — reddish earth, mushroom beds, herb gardens fed by blood in the soil (organ-donor / battlefield runoff, not “he kills patients”). Near a river (he washes only in still water).

Why hurt: Casualties left behind after Summer Court vs Gloaming Court fighting north of the Gray Reach. He dragged them here.

Dart wrinkle: Residual positive charge in Dart’s body (patient #01). Mid-run EMP / charge spike — continuity from Pale Ring, not the cause of the ward.

Table: Ayla absent. Healers = Ennalyn, Drii, Edmund, Cameron (4). Max 2 patient cards in hand each.

2. Session flow (~3 hours)

  1. Lead-in (10–20 min): Court drama / tip — Drii needs blood/heart look; doctor at Ambervale.
  2. Approach: Red soil, mushrooms, iron-boot tracks, blood-soaked gauze-cap vibes. Players may assume villain; they’re wrong.
  3. Roadside surgery: Barely-alive casualty outside. Telegraph group surgery. They almost certainly use it and succeed.
  4. Entrance: Cameron pops from a mushroom / cabinet / ground. “You’re healers. Scrub in. Interns. Now.”
  5. Deal (above table): He will give the Lann if they save every patient.
  6. Crash course (5–8 min): He teaches the 8 item-abilities; divvy ~2 each (including himself).
  7. Hospital loop: Triage board + patient cards + Fear escalations + Dew Garden tells.
  8. Wave ramp: Start smaller; after first 4 cleared, ramp to 10 in play (8 attended max → 2 always unattended).
  9. Modules: Break-room exhaustion, lovers bump, Dart charge/EMP, blood-bank spends on Dry Earth.
  10. Climax: Final simultaneous batch of remaining criticals.
  11. Resolution: Win → heartfelt give + optional arm surgery. Fail paths → fixed outcomes below. Puck exit if Lann leaves him.

3. Win / lose (fairly fixed)

Party / Cameron “succeed”

  • All living patients leave Dry Earth / are Saved.
  • Blood bank may be spent but not emptied fatally at the end (at least 1 pip left) or emptied only as heroic last-act (see arm surgery).
  • Cameron gives Quack freely. Optional: last-act surgery unlocks Edmund’s arms (moderate DC; may spend 1 blood).

Fail / lose Lann

  • Blood bank hits 0: Cameron dies (or ceases); Lann lost/explodes with him — party does not reclaim.
  • Any patient dies while he still has blood: He may try to gift the Lann to the dying sketch (final act). Party must persuade him otherwise (Jordan-twist allowed only as his choice after the fail is real).
  • Beating him up for the Lann is available but boring — telegraphed as the lesser story.

4. Triage stages (Dew Garden)

Pearldew — stable / minor Thirstleaf — delayed Snapped Stem — immediate Dry Earth — expectant

Homage to real MCI: Minor → Delayed → Immediate → Expectant.

  • Pearldew: Holding. Low DC. Can wait.
  • Thirstleaf: Needs care soon. Medium DC.
  • Snapped Stem: Critical. High DC. Priority.
  • Dry Earth: For PCs, treat DC as effectively impossible (~40). Cameron’s Cap Transfusion adds ~+20 and lets him attempt; each attempt costs 1 blood pip.

Saved: Remove card from board to the Saved stack. Counts toward the “first 4 cleared” ramp.

5. Core loop (one turn)

  1. On your turn, you may hold ≤2 patients. Untended patients in play are vulnerable to Fear.
  2. Choose one held patient. Creatively apply an item and/or your own experience/domain ability.
  3. If fiction matches a soft Fey medical truth (GM cheat sheet), grant advantage / +die / lower DC.
  4. Roll Duality (or appropriate DH check). Success → improve one stage (or Saved from Pearldew). Failure → no improve; optional complication.
  5. Fear on the roll → escalate per Fear table.
  6. Cameron may use reaction Bark Advice between PC actions (see score).

7. Fear distribution (~45% of Duality rolls)

Default scheme (use this): When Fear is rolled, escalate one patient:

  1. Prefer an unattended patient in play.
  2. Else prefer Thirstleaf / Snapped Stem over Pearldew.
  3. Else escalate a random held patient (the work got harder mid-procedure).

Pearldew → Thirstleaf → Snapped Stem → Dry Earth.

Soft dial: If the table is crushing it, Fear may escalate all unattended one step (announce once when you flip this dial). If struggling, Fear only ticks unattended Pearldew→Thirstleaf.

8. Blood bank (4 pips)

Public bad-countdown prop. Each Cap Transfusion on Dry Earth: −1 pip, +~20 to that attempt.

At 1 pip: telegraph death. At 0: he dies; Lann gone. Players should fear this like Denton’s meter.

He may stubbornly re-spend on the same doomed patient (foil: “always worth it”) while PCs argue triage ethics.

9. Modules (all core)

Break room

After each wave, stack exhaustion (−1 / stacking disadvantage on checks). An action spent resting in the break room clears exhaustion for that healer for the rest of the encounter (first rest reveals the rule). Cameron never rests; he trash-talks the break room as reverse telegraph.

Lovers

Patients #07 Lark-Split Banner (Summer) and #12 Ash-Under-Snow (Gloaming) share mirrored sensory tells. If the party seats them adjacent / treats them as a pair / names the bond, both jump one stage better immediately (once).

Dart charge / EMP

Patient #01 still carries Pale Ring charge. Hint: dangerous to the ward. Edmund/Cameron foil if someone says dump him. Mid-run: EMP drops every in-play patient one stage (or surge his personal meter). Negating charge = lean into “negative” thoughts / roleplay (callback).

Climax batch

When ≤4 remain unsaved (or blood ≤2), declare Code Dew: all remaining must be stabilized this round/wave or they slide toward Dry Earth. Encourage cross-pass of patients.

Edmund arm surgery

On good ending with ≥1 blood left: Cameron may attempt one surgery to restore Edmund’s arms. Moderate DC. May spend last blood for the +20. Success = permanent unlock; fails gracefully if not.

Dew Garden

Required visual/narration. See §11. Dew height = patient prognosis. Nonchalant “my foot hurts” guy still dripping toward Dry Earth.

10. Soft Fey medical truths (GM cheat — do not print for players)

  • Bones answer to pleasant smell (herbs, spice, song, perfume, flowers).
  • Iron / cold wounds hate running river water; prefer stillwater / bowl.
  • Summer patients brighten under warm candle/orange light metaphors; Gloaming under blue/night candle.
  • Odd counts soothe Red Cap superstition (3 sutures, 1+2 leeches, etc.).
  • Open wounds care that they’re closed more than how (Sealing Pitch comedy).
  • Head / memory / spirit pressure → Trepaner’s Spool / extract.
  • Bad humors / “poison blood” → Blue-Wing Leeches.
  • Finding the hidden hurt → Pointing Reed first, then treat.

11. Dew Garden — narration tools

Enter blurb:

Beyond the mushroom ring, a plot of pale grass drinks the red soil. Every blade wears a bead of morning dew — even at dusk — and the beads do not fall equally. Some shine fat and high. Some cling halfway. Some are already sliding toward the dirt. If you lean close, each bead holds a tiny reflection of a face in the ward.

During play: When a patient worsens, narrate their bead slipping. When Saved, the bead soaks into the blade and the grass stands taller. Cameron’s morning ritual: he walks the rows to remember why he won’t quit.

Table prop: Optional small tray of grass/wheat stalks with water beads, or just print the Dew card and point at stages on the triage board as “dew height.”

13. Balance dials (3-hour)

  • Expected blood spends: 1–3 if they triage well; 4 = tragedy ending.
  • EMP once, mid-late, not opener.
  • If >30 min ahead of pace, spawn an extra Thirstleaf pair from “late stretchers.”
  • If drowning, quiet a Fear escalation or reveal one soft truth aloud via Cameron’s Bark Advice success.
