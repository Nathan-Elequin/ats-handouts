  • Triage names: Stable / Delayed / Urgent / Expectant. DC steps: Delayed +1d6, Urgent +1d6, Expectant +4d6. Patients arrive at printed start triage (01–06 Delayed; most 07–14 Urgent). Presenting problem only until the fold reveals escalation.
  • Admission (canonical): Redcaps dragged the wounded here. Once on the grass, dew moves them like a conveyor — positions each patient on a toadstool-cap bed per the doctor’s orders. Healers never leave the ward; don’t invent new arrival gags per card.

Deal geometry: Waiting Area (v2.2)

Goal: chaos of a real ward — short read window, frantic reassignment, hard capacity.

  1. Four healers. Each starts the round with a hand / ward of exactly 2 full patient cards (face-up in front of them).
  2. 30 seconds: read your own two cards only. No formal table-wide study.
  3. Nathan counts down aloud from 30. During that count, players may pass:
    • Place a card into the shared center Waiting Area and ask someone to take it.
    • You may not place cards into another player’s ward / hand area.
    • A player who wants a Waiting Area card must take it themselves into their own ward.
    • Capacity 2: if your ward is full, you must first put one of yours into the Waiting Area before taking another.
  4. When the count hits 0: anything still in the Waiting Area is untreated this round (Fear magnet / Overflow).
  5. Then treat: Stick + seat tools + abilities. Duality rolls as usual.

Kanban: when a patient is in play, its token (name + icon) sits under the triage column. Full card stays with whoever is treating them. On escalate / quiet / flare / discharge, slide or remove the token — not the hand card.

Round structure

  1. Fear / Dew / blood bookkeeping from last round.
  2. Deal / top-up: each healer to 2 cards in ward (draw from deck / ramp pile). Place matching kanban tokens under their start triage column.
  3. 30s read → 30-count pass via Waiting Area → leftovers = untreated.
  4. Each healer with 1–2 patients: Stick + seat items + own abilities; Duality rolls.
  5. Cameron may Bark Advice / Cap Transfusion / Never Rest as before.
  6. Modules (break room, lovers, charged-quickling EMP, climax) unchanged in intent.

Triage

Stable — base DC · can wait Delayed — +1d6 · soon Urgent — +1d6 · now Expectant — +4d6 · dire

Each step from Stable adds those dice to the treatment DC (Expectant is the cliff). Cap Transfusion +~20, costs 1 blood pip (bank of 4).

Success quiets a presenting/escalation problem and pushes triage toward Stable. Nothing is permanently solved — Fear rolls can flare quieted problems. Discharge only when you decide they are Stable enough to leave the board. Win condition: keep everyone alive / Stable.

The Stick (universal)

Every healer forages / is “chosen by” a plain wood stick within five feet of the ward. Cameron hyperexaggerates it: wisdom-on-contact, medicinal destiny, never for sale.

Truth: it’s a poking stick. Diagnosis via poke. Optional prop: d6 “charges” that count down on use — nothing happens at zero except maybe it snaps after the 6th poke (comedy).

Not one of the eight seat items.

Eight seat items (Feywild artifacts)

Place two item cards at each of four seats. Players grab nearest. Cards are first-impression only (fantasy name + makeup / sense / handle-it). No purpose text. Purpose learned by trial on patients.
