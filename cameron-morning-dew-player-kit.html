Ambervale Ward Rulesv2 · Throw → Claim · obfuscated tools

The deal

Help clear the ward. If everyone is Saved, the Red Cap gives you The Morning Dew (Quack). He’s your attending — chaotic ally, not a boss fight.

Every round

  1. You are dealt patient cards (hands may be uneven).
  2. 30 seconds to read. Tools at your seat stay face-up.
  3. Countdown from 10. On zero: throw all patients to the center (you cannot keep).
  4. Claim: grab up to 2. If two people grab the same card, it goes to Overflow (untreated). Leftovers = untreated.
  5. On your turn, treat a claimed patient with your Stick, seat implements, and/or your own abilities. Persuade Nathan. Roll.
Tools: Descriptions are vague on purpose. Learn by trying them on patients. There is no answer key — only “that’s clever enough.”
