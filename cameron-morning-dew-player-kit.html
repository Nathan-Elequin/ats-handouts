Ambervale Ward RulesPlayer reference · hand to the table

The deal

Help clear this field hospital. If everyone is Saved, the Red Cap gives you the Lann The Morning Dew (Quack). Work with him — he’s your attending, not your boss fight.

On your turn

  • Hold at most 2 patient cards. Pass extras.
  • Pick one held patient. Describe how you use a taught implement and/or your own abilities.
  • Roll the check Nathan calls. Success → patient improves one stage (or Saved from Pearldew).
  • Patients not in anyone’s hand are unattended and take Fear hits first.

Dry Earth: Don’t be surprised if the math says “not worth it.” The Red Cap disagrees. When he squeezes his cap, he’s spending his own life (public blood pips). If those hit zero, bad things.

Soft clue example (not a real ward solution)

If you ever notice that pleasant smells seem to help certain structural hurts, share it. Discoveries stack for the whole table. There are other Fey medical truths — find them by doing, not by asking for a spoiler list.

Other ward facts

  • After the first stretchers clear, more arrive — expect up to 10 in play.
  • Exhaustion stacks as the shift wears on. Clever healers may notice a break room.
  • Some patients can talk. Listen. War stories are not flavor only.
  • One patient still crackles with Pale Ring charge. That’s a problem for later.
