Player ref · Neighbor Seams

Goal

Save everyone. Then the Red Cap keeps his word.

Assign patients

  1. 2 cards between you & each neighbor (left + right seams).
  2. 30s — read your seams only (not the whole table).
  3. Countdown from 10. Per seam: grab one, other goes to your neighbor.
  4. Same grab? Both roll. Winner takes it. Loser takes the leftover or refuses → Overflow (untreated).
  5. Clean round = 2 patients (one from each seam). Then treat.

Treat

  • Stick + seat tools + your abilities. Pitch to Nathan. Roll.
  • Tools are vague on purpose — learn by trying them.
  • Success usually improves one triage step. Saved = off the board.

Fear & escalation

  • Fear prefers Overflow / untreated first, then Gold & Scarlet.
  • Escalation = a patient worsens one step (toward Ink).
  • Refusing a leftover feeds Overflow — and feeds Fear.
  • When the ward is crushing it, more patients may escalate at once.

Ink ≈ nearly impossible alone. Watch the Red Cap’s countdown.
