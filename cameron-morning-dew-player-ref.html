Print 100% · cut on borders. 4 identical refs. Waiting Area pass · capacity 2.
Ambervale Ward
Player ref · Waiting Area

Goal

Save everyone. Then the Red Cap keeps his word.

Hand & pass

  1. You start each round with a hand of 2 patient cards (your ward).
  2. 30 seconds — read your hand. Chaos is the point.
  3. Nathan counts down from 30. During that count you may pass.
  4. To pass: put a card into the center Waiting Area and call for someone to take it.
  5. You may only place cards into the Waiting Area — never into another player’s ward.
  6. Only you may take a card from the Waiting Area into your ward.

Capacity 2. Full? Drop one into Waiting Area before you take another.

Treat

  • Stick + seat tools + your abilities. Pitch to Nathan. Roll.
  • Each card shows one presenting problem. Fold flap hides escalation until Nathan says so.
  • Success quiets a problem and pushes toward Stable — not permanent. Fear can flare quieted problems. Goal: keep everyone alive / Stable.
  • Kanban token slides on triage; hand card stays in your ward while you treat.

Triage

Stable base
Delayed +1d6
Urgent +1d6
Expectant +4d6

Fear & escalation

  • Cards left in the Waiting Area when the count ends = untreated / Fear bait.
  • Fear prefers untreated first, then Delayed & Urgent.
  • Escalation = worsen one step (toward Expectant).

Expectant ≈ nearly impossible alone (+4d6). Watch the Red Cap’s countdown.
